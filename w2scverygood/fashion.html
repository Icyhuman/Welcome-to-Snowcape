The ☆★Shi~ne★☆ sweater

In japanese, "死ね" is a command to die. In english, it's a command to sparkle brightly, and to always be yourself. At the intersection of these two meanings, the ☆★Shi~ne★☆ design takes the darkness of death, and drowns it out with the spark of life.

The SIGNATURE series

A beloved Snowcape tradition, the SIGNATURE series was established as a way for residents to leave their mark on the town, even after moving away. From what was once a handful of names drawn near the town entrance, the island's SIGNATURE walkway is now on the verge of needing a third expansion. With a "no friend is forgotten" policy, some residents who moved out prior to the SIGNATURE collection being established were even invited back for a visit, to make sure their role in Snowcape's grand rise from a humble village to the hot tourist destination it is today does not go forgotten.
